About me

Let's get acquainted...

    Since the age of 16 I have worked for myself. My first project: an online headphone store in 2013. Created a website and a group in VK. Filled it with content, promoted the group and processed orders. In 2014, I sold an online store for $ 520. At the same time, I liked to read a lot and moonlighted as a copywriter for about a year.

    In 2014 I entered the University of BarSU as a software engineer, studied for 2.5 years. I understood that at that stage of my life I was more interested in entrepreneurship and creating my own projects, but there was not enough time for everything. I decided to transfer to distance learning in order to devote more time to projects. But I was drafted into the army, served at the border for 1.5 years (2016-2018). In the army, I learned endurance, organization and received many lessons from life, tried to get the maximum out of the army.

    In 2018, I returned home. Together with a partner, we have created a new project in the field of MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) equipment. I created the site MrtMed24 and was engaged in its Seo promotion (now there are more than 100 key queries in the top 5, in Yandex).

Along with the previous project, I created an Instagram store BlackBert_shop. Handmade wood products. The store has been sold.

 Why me?

Why exactly should I work in your team?

I like to present my ideas and communicate with people.

The development of leadership qualities in myself is of great importance to me. Developing yourself as a leader makes it possible not to stay at the same level (thereby degrading), but to grow personally, bringing more and more benefits to the company, other people and yourself.

The work of a programmer is a constant problem solving.

I would call it "Obstacle Race", where you follow a straight line for some time and it is easy for you due to experience and baggage of knowledge. But then another obstacle is encountered that must be overcome. And this is where character comes in.

With deep analysis, everything lends itself to logic.

Even the most difficult programming problems have a logical solution. I always like to solve complex and interesting problems. Especially if everything is decided simply and beautifully.

Time-management is a very familiar term for me.

I appreciate my own and other people's time. Improve my multitasking skills. I know how to abstract and concentrate when solving tasks

Sport – mine.

I lead a healthy and active lifestyle, I am ready to take part in such events and win 😉. Played MMA, football, swimming, billiards and tennis.

Modern development is largely a team story.

To succeed in it, soft skills are very important for the ability to find a common language with colleagues and partners, to negotiate.
